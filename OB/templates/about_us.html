About Us

Our Team

Andrew Lee and Abigail Rawlins are the creators of Okay Bloomer. We started this project because we both love indoor plants and technology. We wanted to make a website to help people like us who sometimes forget to take care of their plants.

Why We Started Okay Bloomer

We noticed that there were a few online resources about plant care that inspired us. We thought it would be fun and useful to create our own tool to make it easier to remember to water and look after our plants.

What We Offer

At Okay Bloomer, we provide tools and features to help you care for your indoor plants with ease. To learn more about what we offer, please visit our Services page.
